# Pinging

<img width="261" height="343" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/94713cb7-98f9-4d5e-9104-db901803f2fd" />

Binding Ping (Radio Message Wheel) to **{5}** and Delete Marker to **{4}** enables the use of thumb to mark/unmark without impairing movement or shooting.

# Vehicle

<img width="1443" height="737" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f687ee49-5a9c-4b9a-a920-7df5eca807ce" />

* With Driver Seat bound to **{W}** and **{Shift + W}** you can switch seats and start driving immediately from any position in the car
* Binding Seat 2 to **{E}** and Seat 3 to **{Q}** enables quick access to both sides of the vehicle for drive-by shooting
* In a drive-by leaning out from a window opposite to the enemy significantly reduces the exposed hitbox
